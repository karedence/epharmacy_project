About Our System

The Interactive E-Pharmacy Management System simplifies medicine procurement by allowing patients to search medicine prescribed by the doctor and locate pharmacies with the required medications.

Pharmacies can also manage inventory effectively while leveraging machine learning for demand forecasting.

Features of the System

• Patient and Pharmacist Accounts
•Medicine Inventory Managementwith CRUD
•Interactive Dashboard with Analytics
